Direct answer

An AI data analyst job description should combine analytics fundamentals with AI workflow skills. The role includes defining business questions, validating data, using AI tools, reviewing outputs, communicating findings, and maintaining trust in automated analysis.

Point of view

The AI data analyst role is not prompt writing. It is analysis ownership in a team where AI handles more of the mechanical workflow.

Role summary template

Role summary: The AI Data Analyst uses AI-assisted analytics systems to turn business questions into validated analysis, charts, reports, and recommendations. The role owns problem framing, output review, data quality checks, and stakeholder communication.

Responsibilities

  • Clarify business questions and decision context.
  • Define metrics, dimensions, and data quality requirements.
  • Review AI-generated plans, queries, charts, and summaries.
  • Validate outputs against trusted sources and known business logic.
  • Document assumptions, caveats, and correction feedback.
  • Communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders.

Tools this role may use

The role may work with warehouses, BI tools, spreadsheets, notebooks, and AI data analyst platforms. In InfiniSynapse-led teams, the analyst can review the agent's plan, inspect generated operations, and convert results into charts or reports.

What not to put in the job description

Do not define the role as 'prompt engineer for dashboards.' That undersells the real work. The role needs judgment, validation, stakeholder management, and governance awareness.

Evaluate InfiniSynapse with a real analysis workflow

Bring one real question, one database or file, and one trusted reviewer. Use the agent plan, source trace, and final output to decide whether AI-native analysis fits your team.

FAQ

What is an AI data analyst job description?
An AI data analyst job description defines a role that uses AI systems to accelerate analysis while retaining human responsibility for validation and decisions.
What are AI data analyst responsibilities?
AI data analyst responsibilities include framing business questions, checking data quality, reviewing AI outputs, validating results, and communicating findings.
Does an AI data analyst need SQL?
An AI data analyst benefits from SQL literacy because reviewing generated queries and understanding joins, filters, and metrics improves trust in the work.
How should teams hire for an AI data analyst role?
Teams should hire for analytical judgment, business communication, data validation habits, and comfort working with AI tools rather than prompt writing alone.
